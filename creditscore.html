What is my Credit Score?

How is my score calculated?


Your credit score is calculated based on what's in your credit report. Your credit report catalogs actions such as:
• The amount of money you’ve borrowed.
• The number of credit applications you’ve made.
• Whether you pay on time.

How is my score measured?

Depending on the credit reporting agency, your score will be between zero and either 1,000 or 1,200. Here at Felicity Bank, we use 1000.
The score relates to a five-point scale (excellent, very good, good, average and below average). This helps a lender work out how risky it is for them to lend to you.
